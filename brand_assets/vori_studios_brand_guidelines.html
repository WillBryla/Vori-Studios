Do's and Don'ts

Do

Use navy as the primary background. Use mint for key text and accents only. Keep plenty of white space. Use Montserrat exclusively.

Don't

Don't use bright blue, gold, or maroon. Don't mix fonts. Don't use drop shadows on the wordmark. Don't put the logo on busy backgrounds.

Do

Always maintain strong contrast. The wordmark on navy or white only. Keep tagline smaller than the wordmark at all times.

Don't

Don't stretch or distort the logo. Don't use all caps for body copy. Don't use the monospaced or serif fonts anywhere in brand materials.

Brand Voice

Direct. Say what you mean. No filler words.
Confident. You know your craft. Act like it.
Outcome-focused. Clients want results, not aesthetics lectures.
Clean. No jargon. No corporate speak. No buzzwords.
