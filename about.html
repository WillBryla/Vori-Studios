Background

How I got into this.

Freshman year I signed up for engineering because it sounded interesting. I didn't expect to spend the next four years in the SystemsGo curriculum — a program that takes you from basic engineering concepts all the way through designing and launching a real rocket. Hands-on, no shortcuts.

The coding side clicked early. I started building things outside of class — tools, utilities, and eventually websites. I noticed that a lot of small businesses in town had outdated sites or none at all, and agencies were quoting them $8,000 to fix it.

I figured I could do better. So I started Vori Studios.
